Do you offer a warranty on your appliances?
Yes. All Maxwell appliances come with a comprehensive warranty. Our service team is available to assist with any issues — whether it's a repair, replacement, or technical question. We're just a call or email away, anywhere you are.
Where can I buy Maxwell appliances?
Maxwell appliances are available through our official website, authorized dealers, and leading retail chains across 50+ countries. Visit our website to find the nearest dealer or shop directly online with doorstep delivery.
Do you offer bulk or corporate purchase options?
Yes. Maxwell offers special pricing and customized solutions for bulk buyers, corporate offices, hotels, and real estate developers. Contact our B2B sales team to discuss your requirements and get a tailored quote.
Are Maxwell appliances energy efficient?
Absolutely. All Maxwell appliances are designed with energy efficiency as a core priority. Our products meet international energy standards and are built to reduce electricity consumption — saving you money while protecting the environment.
How do I register my Maxwell appliance?
